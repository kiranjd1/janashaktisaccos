Our Saving Schemes

Normal Saving

This saving acts as primary saving account for the members who can deposit and withdraw from this account as often as they need.

Janashakti Super Fixed Deposit

This scheme allows members to deposit a lump sum amount for a fixed period at a higher interest rate. It is ideal for those who want to earn more on their savings.

Recurring Deposit

This scheme encourages regular savings by allowing members to deposit a fixed amount every month. It is suitable for those who want to build a savings habit.

Child Saving

This scheme is designed for parents who want to save for their children's future education and needs. It offers attractive interest rates and benefits.

Aajivan Pension Saving

Members are required to periodically deposit in this account for a certain time. After the account's maturity they recieve their saving as regular pension till their lifetime.

Special Saving

A special type of normal saving, requires certain minimum balance, higher interest returns.
