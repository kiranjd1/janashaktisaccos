Our Introduction

Established in 2014 A.D., Janashakti Saving and Credit Cooperative is dedicated to providing its members the best possible services with the vision of a beautiful society with secure financial self-reliance and prosperity. Our main goal is to uplift the financial backgrounds of our members and their communities by initiating healthy saving habits and utilizing our credit facilities in various productive sectors.
